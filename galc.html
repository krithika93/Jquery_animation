Profile

M/S. Vijay Associates , ( a properietorship concern ) in the construction industry was formed in the year 1989. Our main objective is to build dwelling units to cater the needs of the MIG( Medium income group) & HIG( Higher income group ) segment. However in a span of 2 years of the beginning the firm, it got the reputation of building premium flats in key residential areas in the city of Chennai.

The total number of projects promoted by thr firm as of now is 39 one under construction and three proposed project/ The firm is well known for its quality construction and choicest design with respect to both exterior and interior structure for the units.
